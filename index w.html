do you know how to canvert mixed number in to an inproper number?

For example 3 5/2 how to canvert it in to an inproper number,

to canvert it you have to multiply 2(denominator) by 3(whole number) = 6,

now add 5(numerator) in the answer 6 = 11,

now replace 5(numerator) by the answer 11 and 2(denominator) will remain as same,

the answer is 11/2,

as you can see the 11/2 in the answer the numerator is bigger than denominator so now it is an inproper number.
